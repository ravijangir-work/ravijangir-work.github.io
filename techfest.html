ABOUT

Techfest, IIT Bombay is Asia's Largest Science and Technology festival & a non-profit organization, with 1,75,000+ people in attendance, a social media reach of 3.1 Million Facebook followers, and a reach across over 2500 colleges across India and over 500 overseas. It provides a platform for one and all to witness one of the most beautiful and groundbreaking amalgamations of science and technology with pure delight and enthusiasm.

The students of IIT Bombay, with a three-tier team structure, manage it entirely. The student team comprises nearly 150 members and runs by 22 heads.

MY ROLE

Creatives Manager | April 2020 - May 2021

Led a 2-tier team of 50+ IITians for crafting and executing the design strategy of the first-ever virtual festival (Due to Covid-19). Curated and designed over 200+ eye-catching digital posters to publicize Techfest. Spearheaded the design of 3 unique theme-based websites that witnessed an overall outreach of 6.2 million+ hits.

Creatives Coordinator | March 2019 - April 2020

As part of the team, I designed 50+ offline and online communication materials to showcase different events and attract participation while coordinating and organizing national-level events.

Web Design

During my period at Techfest, I revamped the whole Enigma website to drive engagement and acquire new users. This increased the number of sign-in through this game by 195% as compared to last year.

What is Enigma?

As the creative manager of Techfest, I designed Enigma, an exciting crypt hunt, an epic fun and joy for the people who are locked and bored in their homes due to the Covid-19 lockdown. It has four rounds from decoding texts and symbols to playing with numbers.

Why to redesign?

UI ISSUE

Enigma is a game to engage the people who are getting bored in lockdown. The white background with a black text over it, makes it a dull website. It needs to be visually attractive from a UI perspective.

UX ISSUE

After talking to past teams, I got to know that the players used to call them to ask a lot of questions related to the website and had a hard time navigating to know the basic rules of the game.
